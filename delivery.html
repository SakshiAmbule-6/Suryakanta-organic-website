FAQs

How do I place an order?

You can place an order on our website by selecting the items you want and proceeding to checkout.

What should I do if there is an issue with my delivery?

If you encounter any issues with your delivery, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will do our best to resolve the issue promptly.

What should I do if I'm not home during the delivery time?

If you're not home during the delivery time, our delivery partner will attempt to leave the package in a safe place or with a neighbor. You can also provide specific delivery instructions during checkout.

Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?

Unfortunately, we cannot change the delivery address once the order is placed. Please ensure the correct address is provided at the time of ordering.

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery charges vary based on your location and order size. Please check the delivery information during checkout for details.
